Why Learn JavaScript?

Based on your lecture syllabus, JavaScript is essential because:

  • It makes websites interactive rather than just static text.
  • It is interpreted, meaning the browser runs it directly without compilation.
  • It is object-based, allowing you to model real-world concepts using properties and methods.
  • It has a vast ecosystem supporting modern web applications.
