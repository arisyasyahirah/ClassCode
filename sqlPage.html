Why Learn SQL?

SQL is the industry standard for communicating with database systems. It is essential for:

  • Handling large amounts of structured data
  • Powering back-end systems for web and mobile apps
  • Data analysis and business intelligence reporting
  • Securing and organizing organizational information
  • Designing scalable systems through Normalization
